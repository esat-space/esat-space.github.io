Your payload's ticket to space

eSat is a femtosatellite platform, designed to make access to space affordable. While we created the platform, you can design and fabricate your own sensor payload module or choose from our collection.

Learn more ▼

Design or choose sensor payload

We provide you the template and support to create and fabricate a custom payload PCB. The circuit board will be added on top of our validated main satellite body. Possible payload choices could be a camera, a spectrometer or a cosmic particle detector.

Code

We develop an Eclipse-based SDK that helps you to program your satellite the way we want to. To assist, we have a comprehensive framework of predefined housekeeping functions.

Testing

Before Launch, the software and hardware has to be reviewed and tested. Because we the main platform is fixed and tested, we will take care of the payload validation. For education, student testing campaigns with our partner labs are a possibilty.

Liftoff

After the integration, we launch 100's of Esat's in a cubesat with a comercial launch provider into Low-earth-orbit. The satellites will last there for up to 3 months, depending on the surface-to-mass ratio.
