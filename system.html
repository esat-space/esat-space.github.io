System

How and what we design to reach orbit

Carrier Satellite

The carrier satellite is designed by our team. We have housekeeping, attitude determination, power management, telecommunication and powerful processing on board.

Your payload

The payload is the part of the satellite that fullfills its purpose. The carrier satellite supports the payload with electricity, communication and processing capabilitie. We provide you with different sensing payloads to fly or test. We strongly advise you to be creative and design your own payload and provide you a hardware development kit (HDK). An requirement for this is that the payload has the same or a smaller outline than the carrier satellite to be compatible. The HDK includes the header locations and design limitations.

Development kit with ground station

Depending on your desired level, we will provide you with a customized development kit, including the satellite engineering model (EM) and the flight model (FM). We designed the engineering model (EM) to be convertible to a ground station support after launch.

Space Transportation System

To keep the price low, we integrate 100's of small satellites and launch them with a commercial provider. In orbit, we first seperate our mother satellite and then deploy all small satellites.
